This Project is based up on the individual financial tracking. It includes day to day expense tracking, data visualization and limiting the budget controls system.

Advantges Over Limitations...

1. Better Control Over Money
finance tracker helps you understand how much money you earn and where you spend it. This gives you complete control over your finances and prevents unnecessary expenses.

2. Clear Expense Management
All expenses are categorized (food, travel, bills, shopping, etc.), making it easy to see spending patterns and identify areas where you can cut costs.

3. Effective Budgeting
You can set daily, weekly, or monthly budgets. The app alerts you when you are close to exceeding your budget, helping you manage money wisely.

4. Tracks Savings and Investments
Finance trackers allow you to monitor savings, fixed deposits, mutual funds, stocks, and other investments in one place, showing profits and losses clearly.
